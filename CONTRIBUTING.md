# Contributing Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing to my project! I welcome contributions from anyone who is interested in helping us improve our project.

Before you get started, please take a moment to review our guidelines:

## Getting Started

There's a verity options to contribute to MyIntro.

### Features

---

You may open an issue with adding –[Feature] to the title, there’s already a template for features.

Requested features will go under review by the developers and if approved the feature will be implemented if not the issue will be closed, and the feature will be declined for the meanwhile.

Duplicates will be removed.

### Bugs

---

There's already an issue template you can use to create your issue! Issues are much appreciated and help MyIntro improve and become a better tool!

## Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about the contribution process, please contact DeveloperCron.

Thank you for your contributions!
